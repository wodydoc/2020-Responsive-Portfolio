Takeaways

Initial Challenges

First time working as the sole developer for a major project where the design and UX decisions were completely up to me

Managing my time between design, development, research and testing, etc. without losing sight of the big picture

Appealling specifically to high schoolers (ages 14-18) without losing or confusing parents, teachers, and alumni

My Solutions & Learnings

If a logo is already recognizable among it's following, designers should not abandon that look, but rather enhance it

Began incorporating the technique of time-blocking into my everyday, allowing me to more effectively juggle my many different responsibilities

Prepare for the worst, hope for the best - absolutely anything can happen, such as a global pandemic, which may cause you to need to change a project completely
